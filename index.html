What is openage?

openage is a free and open-source game engine for developing sprite-based RTS-games.


While initially thought of as reimplementation of the Genie engine it is now far more than that. It has a lot of improvements over the 20-year old components, adds completely new features on top and provides better performance due to different design decisions being made and using modern technology while implementing everything from scratch.
